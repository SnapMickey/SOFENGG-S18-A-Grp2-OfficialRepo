How to reserve for an individual

Look for available PC:

Step 1: Go to Reserve page located on the upper right of the website.

Step 2: Select the Building and Room/Floor you are on.

Step 3: Choose the date and time of the individual's reservation.

Step 4: Click the check box beside the word Individual.

Step 5: Enter the Id number and name of that individual.

Step 6: Click the Find PC button.

Selecting the PC to reserve:

Step 1: Click one of the rows that appeared after clicking the Find PC button.

(It will show a green highlight to the chosen row)

Step 2: Click the "Reserve" button on the bottom right of your screen.

Step 3: Click "Yes" on the confirmation that appeared.

How to reserve for an Event

Look for available Room:

Step 1: Go to Reserve page located on the upper right of the website.

Step 2: Select the Building and Room/Floor you are on or the prefered location the person wants.

Step 3: Choose the date and time of the class/event's reservation.

Step 4: Click the check box beside the word Individual.

Step 5: Enter the Id number, name of the supervisor and name of the event.

Step 6: Click the Find PC button!

Selecting the Room to reserve:

Step 1: Click one of the rows that appeared after clicking the Find Room button.

(It will show a green highlight to the chosen row)

Step 2: Click the "Reserve" button on the bottom right of your screen.

Step 3: Click "Yes" on the confirmation that appeared.

Cancel a reservation

Step 1: Go to Dashboard page located on the upper right of the website.

Step 2: Go to User Reservations or Lab Reservations and look for the name of the individual.

Step 3: Click the color red 'X' button on the same row of the name of the individual

Step 4: Click "Yes" on the confirmation that appeared.

Confirm Reservation Arrival

Step 1: Go to Dashboard page located on the upper right of the website.

Step 2: Go to User Reservations or Lab Reservations and look for the name of the individual.

Step 3: Click the empty checkbox on the same row of the name of the individual

Step 4: Click "Yes" on the confirmation that appeared.

Refresh the dashboard display

Step 1: Go to Dashboard page located on the upper right of the website.

Step 2: Click the refresh button on the upper right of the page.
