How to reserve a PC

Look for available PC:

Step 1: Go to Reserve page located on the upper right of the website.

Step 2: Select a Building then the Room/Floor you prefer.

Step 3: Choose the date of your reservation and time.

Step 4: Click the Find PC button!

Selecting the PC you want to reserve:

Step 1: Click one of the rows that appeared after clicking the Find PC button.

(It will show a green highlight to the chosen row)

Step 2: Click the "Reserve" button on the bottom right of your screen.

Step 3: Click "Yes" on the confirmation that appeared.

Confirm your Reservation to the room Admin:

Step 1: Approach the admin in the room.

Step 2: Tell the admin that you have arrived to use the PC.

How to cancel a reservation


Step 1: Go to Home page located on the upper right of the website.

Step 2: Look at the Pending Reservation part of the Home.

Step 3: If there is an ongoing reservation, click the "Cancel" button.

Step 4: Click "Yes" on the confirmation that appeared.

Reservation Limitations


Amount of Reservation: You can only have 1 Reservation, and you cannot reserve until your current reservation finishes.

Time of each Reservation: 30 minutes.
